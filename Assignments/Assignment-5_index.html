1. Introduction

The Integrated Postsecondary Education Data System (IPEDS) is the primary source for data on colleges, universities, and technical and vocational postsecondary institutions in the United States via the National Center for Education Statistics. This data is downloaded from Tableau Public Resource page and uploaded to my repository on Github. The Github link for the data is: https://github.com/VigneshReddy79/STA553-DataViz/blob/main/Datasets/IPEDS_data.xlsx
The above data has many variables and only few of them are used for visualizations on Tableau. The description of the variables used are:

  • Name: Name of the Institution
  • Highest degree offered: Highest degree offered in each university
  • County name: Name of the County
  • Longitude location of institution
  • Latitude location of institution
  • Admissions total
  • Tuition and fees, 2013-14: Tuition and fees for the year 2013-14
  • State abbreviation: Name of the State
  • Control of institution: Whether the Institution is public or private
  • Degree of urbanization (Urban-centric locale): Whether the university belongs to city/rural/suburb/town
  • Carnegie Classification 2010: Classification of University by Carnegie
  • Total enrollment
  • Full-time enrollment
  • Part-time enrollment
  • Undergraduate enrollment
  • Graduate enrollment
  • Graduation rate - Bachelor degree within 4 years, total

  • Scatter plots showing different kind of enrollments

  • Box-plots showing Tuition fee distribution in four degrees of urbanization
